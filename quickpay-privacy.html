Privacy Policy

App: QuickPay  ·  Developer: no10messi.skr  ·  Last updated: February 2026

QuickPay is a non-custodial, serverless Solana payment app. We do not operate any servers, and we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Overview

QuickPay allows users to send and receive SOL, USDC, and USDT on the Solana blockchain using fixed-amount QR codes. All transactions occur directly on the Solana network. QuickPay never holds, controls, or has access to user funds or private keys.

Data Collection

QuickPay collects no personal information. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other identifying information. No account or registration is required.

Private Keys & Wallet Security

QuickPay does not access your private keys or seed phrase. All cryptographic signing is handled exclusively by the Solana Mobile Seed Vault, a secure hardware element on your device. QuickPay never sees, stores, or transmits your private key.

Blockchain Transactions

When you send or receive a payment, the transaction is broadcast directly to the Solana blockchain. Blockchain transactions are public by nature. QuickPay does not store transaction records on any server — all history is available on the public Solana ledger.

No Servers

QuickPay operates without a backend server. The app communicates directly with Solana RPC nodes solely to submit and confirm transactions. We do not log, store, or analyze any of this activity.

Third-Party Services

QuickPay does not integrate any advertising, analytics, or tracking services. The app communicates directly with Solana RPC nodes (including public endpoints and Helius, a third-party RPC provider) solely to query and submit transactions on the Solana blockchain. No personal data is transmitted to these services.

QuickPay uses the CoinGecko API solely to fetch real-time SOL/USD price data for display purposes. No personal data is sent to CoinGecko. The USD amounts shown in the app are reference values only — actual transactions settle in SOL on the Solana blockchain.

Children's Privacy

QuickPay does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
