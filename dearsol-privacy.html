Privacy Policy

App: Dear. Sol v1.0  ·  Developer: no10messi.skr  ·  Last updated: March 2026

Dear. Sol is a serverless, non-custodial Solana app. We do not operate any servers, and we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external services.

Overview

Dear. Sol allows users to record personal reflections on the Solana blockchain via the SPL Memo Program and receive a "destiny answer" derived from the resulting transaction hash. The app requires a connected Solana wallet (Seed Vault or compatible MWA wallet) to sign and submit transactions. Dear. Sol never holds, controls, or has access to user funds or private keys.

Data Collection

Dear. Sol collects no personal information. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other identifying information. No account or registration is required.

Local Data Storage

Dear. Sol stores the following data locally on your device only (via AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server:

  • Wallet connection info: Your public key (wallet address), MWA auth token, and wallet URI for reconnecting to the same wallet without re-authorization.

You can clear all locally stored data at any time by clearing the app's data through your device settings or by disconnecting your wallet within the app.

Diary Content

Text entered in the diary input field is used solely within the app on your device for the purpose of displaying what you have written. The content you type is not transmitted to the Solana blockchain or any external service. The app submits a short anonymous token to the Solana blockchain for the purpose of generating a transaction hash; your diary text remains on your device only.

Blockchain Transactions

When you use the app, a Solana transaction containing a short anonymous token is broadcast to the Solana blockchain. This transaction is public by nature and visible on-chain. The transaction does not contain any personal information or the content of your diary entry.

Private Keys & Wallet Security

Dear. Sol does not access your private keys or seed phrase. All cryptographic signing is handled exclusively by the Solana Mobile Seed Vault or your connected MWA-compatible wallet. Dear. Sol communicates with the wallet through the Mobile Wallet Adapter (MWA) protocol, which never exposes private key material to the application.

No Servers

Dear. Sol operates without a backend server. The app communicates directly with Solana RPC nodes solely to submit and confirm transactions.

Third-Party Services

Dear. Sol does not integrate any advertising, analytics, or tracking services. The app communicates with the following external services:

  • Solana RPC endpoints (api.mainnet-beta.solana.com): For submitting and confirming transactions. No personal data is transmitted.
  • CoinGecko API (api.coingecko.com): For fetching the real-time SOL/USD price used in the fee display. No personal data is transmitted.

Children's Privacy

Dear. Sol does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
